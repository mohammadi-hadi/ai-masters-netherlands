# Contributing

Corrections and additions are welcome.

## Adding a programme

- Research-university (WO) master's programmes in AI, data science, ML, or closely adjacent fields, taught in the Netherlands.
- One programme per pull request, with: university, official programme name, city, duration, language of instruction, and a link to the **official programme page** (not an aggregator).
- If a programme is a specialisation/track inside a broader master's, say so in the notes.

## Corrections

Programmes get renamed, merged, and capped; fees and deadlines change every year. If something here is out of date, open an issue or PR — the link checker runs weekly, but only official pages are authoritative for deadlines and fees.

## Style

Short, factual, no rankings. Programmes differ by focus and structure, not by a number.
